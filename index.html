What is this?

A question most of us never actually ask ourselves.

We spend a lot of our lives chasing milestones — a better salary, a bigger apartment, a certain number in a bank account. But very few of us have ever stopped to ask: what genuinely makes me happy, right now?

This project starts with one disarmingly simple exercise. And then asks whether the things you listed actually need the money you think they do.

The Core Exercise
"I feel happy when _______________"
drinking coffee ☕ learning something new 📚 talking to a friend 💬 working out 🏋️ listening to music 🎵 making something creative 🎨 playing sports 🏸 watching a good movie 🎬

Most people, when they actually write this list, discover something surprising. The things that genuinely light them up? Most of them are already available to them. Today. Without a raise.

How it works

Three simple sections.
No right or wrong answers.

1

A little about you

Just your age group and life stage. That's it. No name, no email, nothing identifying.

2

Your happiness list

Complete "I feel happy when…" at least 10 times. Big things, small things, silly things — all welcome. Don't overthink it.

3

Three money questions

Quick reflections on how money and happiness connect in your life. No calculations, no judgment — just your honest perspective.
